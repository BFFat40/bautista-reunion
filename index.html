Why I Love Being a Bautista

1. Because Bautistas love food and cook good food! -Gigi Rapadas

2. Because I got to sit in front of the class (arranged alphabetically) and I could see the blackboard even with bad eyes. -Gigi Rapadas

3. Because even if I lost the right to be seated at the front end of class (became an R), I inherited the smarts to comprehend what I don't see quite well on the blackboard. -Eliseo Razon

4. Because of the Bautista love of God. So when Lolo Felix took to heart the bible verse of "go forth and multiply," my generation finds family in almost any corner of the world that welcomes anyone of us to good food while we have a conversation about the same tired old stories that we love to hear and rekindle. -Eliseo Razon

5. Because we care. -Eliseo Razon

6. Lahat may kwento. Lahat may kwenta. -Gregorio Razon

7. Inherited traits: walk, talk like a Bautista long legs, unique smile -Socorro Stanczyk

8. a heartbeat away wherever we are companions on a journey, tugue-there again -Socorro Stanczyk
